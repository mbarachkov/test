Liri Bot

lookup for various entertainment platforms

A simple app made with NodeJS using API's to look up local concert times, movie information, and album information.

*Command List this will give you a response of the soonest concert from your Artist *node liri.js concert-this "Artist" this will give you all of the information about the song you enter *node liri.js spotify-this "Song" this will give you some information about the movie you enter *node liri.js movie-this "Movie"
